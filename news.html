Our next Move

As this year turns into the holiday season, we are thrilled to be highlighting the incredible people behind S.O.U.L. Foundation. From the women and youth we serve to our volunteers, staff, and board members, you will see stories of passionate individuals and lives changed because of S.O.U.L.’s work.
One of the stories I’d like to share with you is featured in our new e-cookbook, “A Community Cooks: Recipes Made with Love from Eastern Uganda.”

Muganda Hassan grew up in the community that we serve and has been with S.O.U.L. since our humble beginnings. He started as teenager as a dedicated volunteer and then became one of our very first sponsored students. In partnership with his family, S.O.U.L. sponsored his university education where he graduated with a degree in aquaculture. Fast forward to now, and Muganda is one of our senior program staff members, and puts his education to use running our fish cage cooperative.

I’m proud of Muganda for many reasons, but most of all for being so dedicated to empowering and leading his community. In addition to S.O.U.L., Muganda runs his own coffee business, Buja Bean. He buys coffee beans from local farmers, many of whom attended our agriculture classes, giving them a fair wage and then skillfully roasts these beans in small batches. He sells his coffee throughout Uganda and the US. Muganda’s recipe in our e-cookbook is for a turmeric honey latte (perfect for the holidays!). With any donation over $25, you will receive a link to download this special book filled with recipes and stories from our community and staff.
Since 2009, we have been inspiring change and providing opportunities for a better future for over 14,000 Ugandans each year. Muganda is one of them. And we can’t wait to show you others. It is indeed the Faces of S.O.U.L. that make us who we are.

In 2020, almost all of our in-person fundraising events were canceled due to COVID-19. But the needs of our community, especially in light of this pandemic, continue to go on. Now, our holiday goal is to raise for our work in Uganda. Our most ambitious goal yet, we hope you'll

JOIN US so that these stories of changed lives may continue for a long time to come.

We can’t thank you enough for standing beside us and with us over the last eleven years. We wish you and yours a safe and heartfelt holiday season. Sincerely,
