Volunteering at Palm Hopes Foundation

We believe everyone deserves the best chance in life. that is why we support the most vulnerable children and their families, working with them to reduce poverty and meet their basic needs.

Training Mothers to Make Necklaces

Sheema is known for its hospitality and jewelers industry has more than 400 slums out of which only a handful are identified by the Municipal Corporation of sheema. Being the municipality city of uganda, thousands of people enter a new world for a better livelihood. These migrants, plus many other underprivileged, live with their children in illegal handmade shacks, tents, or on roads.

Training Mothers to make Table mats

These activities help mothers develop in mental and physical terms. Palm Hopes will facilitate the organisation of events in coordination with respective Institutions and Schools and local authorities. The event will be organised on the occasion of important days like ‘Mothers day, womens day, national education day, children’s day, etc. For conducting each of these events, Organizing committees and sub-committees composed of members from various stakeholder of this project will be formed.

Training Soccar for Social Change

The goal of the project is to offer an opportunity for maximal development of talent in youth through training and nurturing of their talents in football, in the junior, cadet and senior categories. Most of the players are usually identified through township competitions and street playing arenas. Moreover, the object seeks to establish an exchange visit program between the Sportlight FA and Sport Institution and training centers for coaches and players the Nation over.
